About Me

Who Am i?

Gilberto Davis (born 1997, Panama) is an adventurer and a tech lover. He has been programming since the age of 16 as a hobby driven by passion and the feeling of solving problems with a computer.

In 2017, he was a member of the winning team of the first Technological Scientific Fair held at the headquarters of the University of Technology of Panama in Bocas del Toro.

In 2018, he was awarded with the Cisco CCNA Routing and Switching certification for having successfully completed the course and in 2019 he graduated as an Engineering Technician with a Specialization in Software Development from the Technological University of Panama in the province of Bocas del Toro.

Gilberto is currently developing his undergraduate thesis and looking for an opportunity to improve his knowledge by solving more complex problems with software.
